🚀 FiJob - Job Portal Management System
FiJob is an end-to-end recruitment architecture engineered with ASP.NET MVC 5 and SQL Server. While currently optimized for .NET Framework 4.8, the codebase maintains its original .NET 4.5 compatibility. This solution acts as a centralized hub, aligning talent with opportunity by optimizing vacancy publishing, candidate intake, and organizational hiring workflows.

🌟 Core Functionality
👔 Candidate Interface
Identity Personalization: Construct robust professional summaries, integrate biographies, and attach digital CVs.

Smart Search: Filter through opportunities by utilizing job titles, specific tags, or geographic data.

Progress Monitoring: Observe live updates on submission milestones (Awaiting Review, Shortlisted, Declined, or Interview Requested).

Opportunity Vault: Archive interesting vacancies for future consideration.

Live Alerts: Receive immediate pings whenever a hiring manager updates an application status.

🏛️ Recruiter Dashboard
Organizational Branding: Design and maintain corporate pages featuring custom visual identities and logos.

Vacancy Administration: Generate fresh job advertisements and refine current active posts.

Talent Evaluation: Filter through the applicant pool, access CVs via direct download, and transition candidates through the hiring pipeline.

Meeting Coordinator: Organize technical assessments or cultural fit rounds (Virtual, On-site, or Telephonic) with integrated time slots, briefing notes, and automated participant invites.

⚡ Instant Messaging Gateway
Synchronous Communication: Powered by SignalR WebSockets, facilitating fluid, zero-latency dialogue between recruiters and talent without requiring manual browser reloads.
